Morning Brief

Visual identity system for a daily AI & tech newsletter. Designed for dark environments and mobile-first reading.

v1.0 — April 2026

01 — Brand Essence

Calm clarity in a noisy world

Morning Brief distills the day's most important AI and technology developments into a focused, scannable email. The visual identity reflects this mission: cool, restrained, and effortless to read — especially on a phone screen before the day begins.

Principles

Mobile-first. Dark-native. Cool-toned. Spacious. Scannable. Quietly confident — never loud.

Never

Busy gradients. Light backgrounds. Warm/hot accent colors. Dense text blocks. Visual clutter. Generic AI aesthetics.

05 — Spacing & Layout

06 — Voice & Tone

How We Sound

Morning Brief is written for busy professionals scanning on their phone. The visual identity reinforces this through restraint and hierarchy.

Do

Let whitespace breathe. Use the accent color to guide — not decorate. Keep badges and labels minimal. Let Georgia carry the reading experience.

Don't

Add decorative icons to section headers. Use multiple accent colors. Stack badges. Add borders to everything. Use all-caps for body text.
